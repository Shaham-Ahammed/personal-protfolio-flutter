## Sections

Home – A welcoming landing page with a clean introduction, your name/role, a personal quote, and a CV download option.

About – A concise overview of your background, skills, and professional journey.

Experience – Highlights your work experience, accomplishments, and key projects.

Projects – Showcases your portfolio and mini-projects, complete with relevant links and details.

Contact – Provides multiple ways for visitors to get in touch via email, social profiles, or a contact form.
